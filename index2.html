Mini Biography: Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was a scientist, teacher, author, and former President of India.

Early Life

He was born in Rameswaram and grew up with curiosity, discipline, and love for learning.

Note: Students can replace this person with any personality they admire.

Important Achievement

He played a major role in India’s space and missile programmes.

He is remembered as the People’s President because he inspired millions of students.

One value that made him special was lifelong learning.

Correction Corner

He only worked as a scientist. He was also a teacher, writer, and leader.

Interesting Examples

Science reminds us of formulas like H2O and ideas like 102.

He also encouraged students to become the 1st version of themselves, not a copy of someone else.

Words That Inspire

He once said, Dream, dream, dream.

Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action.

My Reflection

I admire Dr. Kalam because he showed that knowledge, kindness, and hard work can change lives.
